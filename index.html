About
BITS Leadership Summit(BLS 2018) aims to act as a forum for prospective world leaders to engage in purposeful exchanges on topics essential for the future of the nation and the society that we hope to inhabit. With an objective to foster an innovative and necessary exchange of ideas and opinions amongst motivated young minds, this summit is an attempt to leverage the brilliance of the Indian youth and aid them in being the forerunners of change in a thriving India.
